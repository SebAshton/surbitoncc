Surbiton Cycle Club

Suburban thrill seekers unite, do you like sharing a draft? SCC is here to ride as a team, race together and feast on the glory, or cinnabuns, whichever comes first.

We want all members to feel a strong part of the club, to enjoy and take pride in representing SCC and to feel confident in helping the club plant roots in the cycling community. We’re all about enjoying riding together, both in races, as part of a team effort, and on social rides and trips abroad.

Forum

Get involved and join the conversation. Our forum is where we arrange rides, discuss race tactics and share insights. It’s our online version of a clubhouse.

Riding with SCC

Meeting in Kingston and heading out to the Surrey Hills, Kent, Sussex and beyond. The focus is on quality training in disciplined groups, riding safely and considerately.

Racing

You’ll find members of SCC representing the team colours at races across the south east – and beyond. Look out for us at road races, crits, CX, and hill climbs.
